Description

Have you ever wondered, 'what could I have possibly missed this year?' Do you ever just want to know everything that happened this year, just so you don't feel like you missed something. Well, good news for you. This website is made entirely for the purpose of recapping every impactful events in 2020. Starting all the way from January to the end of December, this website will give you all the information that you need.

This is the homepage, the part of the website where you can visit the other pages. Below the title (the giant 2020) is the menubar. It is like a mini homepage. Its purpose is to help you easily traverse the website since the menubar will be present in every page. Below that is the context box, where you are currently reading right now. Another thing in the content box are the links mentioned before. Finally, under all that, is the footer. Since this is an unproffesional free website, there isn't much in the footer other than a link to the contacts page. That is all about the homepage, have fun learning information about 2020.
